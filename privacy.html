Privacy Policy for Split Dual Browser

Effective Date: February 2026

1. Information Collection

Split Dual Browser does not collect, store, or share any personal information from users. The app does not require account registration, login credentials, or personal details.

2. External Websites

This application allows users to load and view third-party websites such as Canva, Gmail, and other services. Any data entered on those websites is handled directly by the respective third-party service providers under their own privacy policies. Split Dual Browser does not access, store, or transmit that information.

3. Local Storage

The app may store session information locally on the user's device to remember open tabs or previously loaded URLs. This data remains on the device and is not transmitted to any external server.

4. Data Sharing

Split Dual Browser does not sell, trade, or share user data with any third parties.

5. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you may contact the developer.
